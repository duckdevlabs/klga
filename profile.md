The Profile area is your progress hub in KLGA. It combines identity, practice consistency, BPM progress, weekly goals, and achievements in one place.

## Opening Your Profile

From the Home screen, tap your **avatar** in the header. You can also reach the profile area from **Settings > Account**.

## Profile Screen

The Profile screen shows:

- **Avatar image** — your profile photo, or initials when no photo is set.
- **Display name** — your chosen public name.
- **Rank badge** — a membership label based on progress level.
- **Days streak** — consecutive practice days.
- **Hours** — total logged instrument practice time.
- **Top BPM** — highest BPM achieved during practice.
- **Weekly goals** — progress against the weekly practice target and active BPM goals.
- **Achievements** — unlocked achievements in full color and locked achievements as muted icons.

## Editing Your Profile

Open the edit action from the Profile screen to update:

- **Profile picture** from your device photo gallery.
- **Display name**.
- **Phone number**.

Email and account identity are managed by authentication and cannot be edited directly from this screen.

## Avatar Upload Fallback

KLGA saves avatar images to cloud storage. If image upload is unavailable, the app still saves supported text changes such as display name and phone number, then shows a warning about the image upload issue.

## Sign Out

The Profile area includes a sign-out action. Signing out returns you to the login flow and preserves your practice data on the server.
