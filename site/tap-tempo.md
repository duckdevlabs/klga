Tap Tempo lets you find a tempo by tapping a rhythm, then send it directly to the metronome.

## How to Use

1. Open the **Tap Tempo** tab from the bottom navigation bar.
2. Tap the large area in the centre of the screen repeatedly at the speed you want.
3. After a few taps the detected **BPM** appears on screen along with a tap count.
4. Keep tapping to refine the average. The more taps you give, the more accurate the result.
5. When you're happy with the tempo, tap the **Play** button below the tap area.

## What Happens Next

Pressing Play does three things:

1. Sends the detected BPM to the metronome.
2. Starts metronome playback automatically.
3. Navigates you to the **Metronome** tab so you can see the beat indicator and adjust settings.

## Tips

- The detected BPM is clamped to the range **20–300**, matching the metronome's supported range.
- If you lose your rhythm, just pause for a moment and start tapping again — the calculation resets.
- Use Tap Tempo when you hear a song and want to match its speed, or when a teacher calls out a tempo by clapping.
