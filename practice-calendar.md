The Practice Calendar helps you build a consistent practice habit by showing which days you've practised and tracking your current streak.

## Viewing the Calendar

Open the **Practice Calendar** tab from the bottom navigation bar. You'll see:

- A **monthly grid** with one cell per day.
- Days you've practised are marked with a check icon.
- The current month and year are shown at the top.

## Navigating Months

Use the left and right **chevron arrows** next to the month title to move between months and review your history.

## Marking a Day

Tap any day in the grid to toggle it:

- Tap an unmarked day to **mark it as practised**.
- Tap a marked day to **unmark it** if you made a mistake.

Changes are saved to the server so they persist across devices.

## Streak

Below the calendar grid a **streak indicator** shows how many consecutive days you've practised:

- If you practised today it shows your current streak count.
- If you haven't practised yet today it reminds you how many days since your last session.

Use the streak as motivation to keep your chain going!

## Automatic Sync

- **Pull down** on the calendar to refresh data from the server.
- When you open the calendar, any **metronome playtime** that was tracked in the background is automatically synced as a practice session for that day. You don't need to manually log it.
- Data syncs to your account, so switching devices shows the same history.

## Home Screen Preview

On the **Home** tab, a **Practice Calendar card** shows a quick week view (Mon–Sun) with dots indicating which days you practised. Tap the card to jump to the full calendar.
